Welcome!

Hi! Welcome to the best plan-your-holidays platform! If you are here, it means you are looking for an unforgettable break.

We will teach you how to search for experiences based on your preferences. We know our customers' likes can be different. That's why we have such a big list of possible preferences. Feel free to contact us if you don't find your preference there.

Weather

To begin, choose the weather you're expecting. You can choose between cold climes and tropical ones, from rainy places to dry-weather ones. Cold with snow or without it. Rainy-tropical cities or dry-tropical cities. What is your preference?

Biomes

Now, let us filter by biomes. You can choose from the most beautiful beaches to the most imponent mountains. You can look from deserts to jungles like the Amazonas Jungle. There are many more biomes to choose from. You can also leave your suggestion if you don't find a specific biome you'd like.

Activities

A very special-recently-added feature is the search by activities. If you'd like to make alpinism, you won't see many beaches on the results page. You can choose mountaineering, rappelling, canoeing, kayaking, taking impressive hikes, surfing formidable waves, being the best in paintball, swimming with dolphins, diving into deep oceans, and much more activities. Of course, you can filter by price also.
