Overview

Purpose

Were you in the situation of trying to find where to go on vacations but run out of ideas? Or you didn't want to do the same activities as always? That's what this is all about! We help people to make their holidays unforgettable for an excellent price. Our web page suggests places according to your preferences. Your preferences may be the distance, temperature, calmness, excitement of the activities, and much more. There you can see pictures of the most beautiful places in the world. Not just that, but there you can find the easiest, the most relaxing, or most exciting way to be there. Reviews don't lie! So we gathered the opinion of one million and a half people so far, to make the decision simple and secure! Even you can contribute and write your reviews!

Audience

As a company, we aim to help people interested in enjoying their free time by traveling and appreciating the beauties of our world. Our model customer is someone who works 40-60 hours a week, and after several months of hard work, he knows that he has a strong desire for ideal holidays. We look to make planning a pretty easy task. Our customers would usually access the site through a desktop computer or notebook.

Typography

Heading Font: Open Sans

I choose Open Sans, because it is a font that seems modern and also sober. In this case I'm combining two similar sans-serif fonts.

Paragraph Font: Montserrat

Montserrat is a sans-serif font, I look for pairing suggestions for Open Sans and I think montserrat is a good modern one.

Content

Home page

Hi! Welcome to the best plan-your-holidays platform! If you are here, it means you are looking for an unforgettable and relaxing break.

We will teach you how to search for experiences based on your preferences. We know our customers' likings can differ a lot. That's why we have such a big list of possible preferences. Feel free to contact us if you don't find your preference there.

To begin, choose the weather you're expecting. You can choose between cold climes and tropical ones, from rainy places to dry-weather ones. Cold with snow or without it. Rainy-tropical cities or dry-tropical cities. What is your preference?

Blog

An especially important part of our website is our blog. There, people can ask questions about any specific place or activity they'd like to take. In the blog, you can also share your experiences. You can describe to everybody many aspects of your trip. Like the weather you enjoyed, the best food you found, the weirdest food you tried, and also share some photos.

Reviews

We've built our site from the opinion of people like you that was generous with their thoughts and gave us constructive feedback. We enjoy reading constructive feedback from our customers. We know it can be frustrating to handle a website that wasn't well-thought for the best user experience. So, your comments are important to us.

Please, if you have some suggestions to make your experience better, don't hesitate to write a review. You're helping thousands of people every day.

First, remember what you were doing when you noticed the problem or the feature that you liked. Second, think of the workers who strive to provide you with the best service. Third, if you had a bad experience, we're sorry, please strive to use respectful language so you don't hurt anyone's feelings. Fourth, remember that constructive comments make everybody's experience better. Five, receive a warm thanks from our part for your help.
